About Me

I am an engineering student deeply focused on the intersection of electric vehicle technology, control systems design, and intelligent mobility. My work spans hybrid energy storage architectures, regenerative systems, and embedded control — all driven by a mission to engineer cleaner, smarter transportation for the future.

I approach every project with a systems-level mindset — from mathematical modeling and simulation to real-time diagnostics and hardware-software co-design.

An electromagnetic regenerative suspension system that harvests kinetic energy from road vibrations and converts it into electrical energy to recharge the EV battery, improving overall energy efficiency.

A PID-based altitude hold controller for quadrotor UAVs, implementing sensor fusion and real-time feedback loops to maintain stable hover altitude under varying disturbance conditions.
